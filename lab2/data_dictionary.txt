Entities:
	- fact_title
		* stores information about titles' names and times of creation
	- dim_rating_type
		* stores information about titles' average ratings and rating types
	- dim_title_type
		* stores information about titles' type of title (movie, short, tvseries, etc.)
	- dim_type_genre
		* stores information about titles' genre

Important Attributes:
	- rating_type stores one of the following values depending on the title's rank: appalling, average, outstanding.
